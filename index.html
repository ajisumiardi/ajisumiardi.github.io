About Me

Teaching Information and Communications Technology at Sekolah Abata Lombok. In detail, teaching from grade 4 to 6 at Abata Lombok Integrated Islamic Elementary School, grade 7 to 9 at Abata Lombok Integrated Islamic Junior High School, and grade 10 to 11 at Abata Lombok Senior High School

Hardware Embedded Engineer means a person who posses various skills to combine software (programs, code, or alghoritms) onto hardware components such as microcontrollers, drive modules, and another electronic hardwares. But in my case, posses C++ Language, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and knowledge about Arduino and ESP board such as Arduino UNO, Arduino Nano, Arduino Mega, ESP8266, ESP32, and ESP32CAM. Those skills bring me to my another role in Sekolah Abata Lombok as Robotic Extracuricular Coach that teach about robotic and programming stuff to grade 2 and 4 pupils.
